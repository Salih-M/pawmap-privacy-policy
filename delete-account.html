PawMap – Account & Data Deletion Request

PawMap allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data in accordance with Google Play policies.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your PawMap account, please send an email to:

salimemis@gmail.com

Use the subject line: PawMap – Delete My Account

Please include:

  • The email address used in the app (if applicable)
  • Your user ID (if available)
  • A confirmation that you want your PawMap account deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, the following data will be deleted:

  • Your account (Firebase Authentication)
  • Your profile information stored in the database
  • Reports and submissions associated with your account
  • Uploaded images linked to your account (where applicable)

Data Retention

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Some system logs or backups may be retained temporarily for security and legal compliance purposes and are automatically deleted within 90 days.
