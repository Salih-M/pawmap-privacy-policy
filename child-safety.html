Child Safety Standards (CSAE / CSAM)

PawMap — Child Safety Standards

Last updated: March 5, 2026

PawMap is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We strictly prohibit any content related to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Our standards

  • Zero tolerance policy: Any CSAM/CSAE content is strictly forbidden.
  • Reporting tools: Users can report inappropriate content or behavior inside the app.
  • Moderation & removal: Reports are reviewed and violating content is removed.
  • Account enforcement: Accounts involved in abuse or illegal activity are permanently banned.
  • Cooperation with authorities: We comply with applicable laws and report confirmed CSAM/CSAE where required.
